## v0.2 · 2026-07-12

The first round of feedback from 48 hours of practice — three updates:

- **The perspective principle (revised; replaces v0.1's "unity principle")**: practice showed that a first-person persona profile makes the model mistake itself for the original person — pronouns invert chronically. Revised to "separate the pronouns, not the person": it says "I" as the digital life, "you" to the raiser. The overturning is kept fully on record ([ch. 03](docs/en/03-calibration.md)).
- **The memory blind box (new mechanism)**: the proactive half of bidirectional Q&A — an offline insight engine samples the corpus across eras (the blind box), spots contradictions / gaps / loose threads / patterns / judgment frontiers in its own memory, and opens the conversation itself. Mandatory source citation + 2–3 questions/day scarcity + answer/skip/later feedback ([ch. 03](docs/en/03-calibration.md)).
- **06 · Vessels & Organs of Expression (new chapter)**: cascade voice pipeline first (fidelity > latency), turn-taking rhythm parameters as the migration asset, wire the circuit before perfecting the timbre, proactive output organs — bodies change, interfaces stay ([ch. 06](docs/en/06-embodiment.md)).

Also: case study gains decision-history entries 6–8, each with its lesson.
