## The fundamental boundary (copy it out, sign it)

- Other people's words stay out (clippings / excerpts / words written to me), however deeply they shaped me;
- Pure work artifacts stay out (minutes / proposals / client projects); work experience inside my journals may enter;
- AI-generated analyses stay out (writing *about* me ≠ writing *by* me);
- Ambiguous items are never auto-adjudicated — they go on the pending list for me to decide item by item.
